My Dear Ewa ❤️ One year. 365 days. 12 months. Countless memories. And somehow, after all this time, I still find myself smiling whenever I think about you. It's funny — if someone had told me back in school in 2020 that the beautiful girl who loved singing Korean love songs, dancing without a care in the world, and laughing in that way only she can laugh would become one of the most important people in my life, I probably wouldn't have believed them. Yet here we are. One year later. Still choosing each other. Still arguing over the smallest things. Still getting annoyed with each other. Still coming back to each other. And honestly? I wouldn't trade that for anything. You know what I've always loved about us? We aren't perfect. We have had those little fights that somehow turn into serious discussions. Moments where we didn't understand each other. Moments where we were both stubborn. Okay... mostly moments where we were both stubborn. 😂 But every single challenge taught me something important: no matter how difficult the moment felt, I never wanted a future that didn't have you in it. Thank you for loving me. Thank you for feeding me when I forget to take care of myself. Thank you for correcting me when I'm doing something wrong, even when I pretend not to listen. Thank you for caring enough to get annoyed sometimes. Thank you for being one of the few people who can make me laugh, think, smile, and reflect all in the same conversation. And can we talk about your laugh for a second? That laugh should honestly come with a warning label. Because once I hear it, I forget what I was angry about. I forget what I was doing. I forget every serious thing on my mind. You laugh and suddenly the whole world becomes lighter. Then there are those random Korean songs. I still don't know what half of them mean. But every time you sing them, you look so happy that I end up smiling anyway. You have this beautiful way of turning ordinary moments into memories. And that's one of my favourite things about you. I don't just love the happy version of you. I love the excited version. The playful version. The emotional version. The stubborn version. The dramatic version. The version that gets annoyed. The version that laughs too hard. The version that dreams big. The version that sometimes overthinks. I love all of you. Every single part. This year gave me memories I will never forget. And if the next year gives me more laughter, more growth, more conversations, more adventures, and more moments with you, then I already know it's going to be beautiful. So here's to one year of us. One year of memories. One year of learning each other. One year of choosing each other. And hopefully many more years of me listening to Korean love songs I don't understand, because the girl singing them means everything to me. Happy Anniversary, my IFE. ❤️ Thank you for an unforgettable year. I love you more than words can fully explain. Forever yours,
Emmanuel ❤️
